**Customer Behavior Analysis**

**Overview**
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to generate actionable business insights. It focuses on identifying revenue trends, evaluating discount effectiveness, and segmenting customers based on demographic factors such as age and gender.

The project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis — from data extraction and transformation to visualization and insight generation — aligned with real-world business decision-making.

**Business Objectives**
Identify key drivers of revenue growth
Evaluate the effectiveness of discount strategies
Segment customers based on demographic attributes
Analyze purchasing patterns to improve targeting and retention
Enable data-driven decision-making through dashboards

**Tech Stack**
Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data cleaning, transformation, exploratory analysis
MySQL – Data querying, joins, aggregations, and data extraction
Power BI – Interactive dashboards and KPI visualization
📂 Project Structure
├── customer_shopping_behavior.csv                # Raw and cleaned datasets

├── Customer-Shopping-Behavior-Analysis.pptx      # Powerpoint
├── Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis.docx      # Descriptive word document
└── README.md

**Key Analysis Performed**
Revenue Analysis: Trend analysis to identify peak sales periods
Customer Segmentation: Behavior comparison across age groups and gender
Discount Impact: Correlation between discounts and purchase volume
Sales Performance: Category-level and customer-level insights
KPI Tracking: Revenue, average order value, and purchase frequency

**Key Insights**
Certain customer segments contribute disproportionately to overall revenue
Discounts increase short-term sales but may impact overall profitability
Specific age groups show higher purchasing frequency and engagement
Gender-based trends reveal differences in product preferences and spending patterns

**Dashboard Highlights**
Interactive revenue and sales trends
Customer segmentation visuals
Discount vs. sales performance analysis
Dynamic filters for deeper exploration
